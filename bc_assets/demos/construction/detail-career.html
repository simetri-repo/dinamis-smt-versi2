Sales Engineer

Job Description

  • Pitching for new client/project opportunity.
  • Providing product, service, or equipment technical and engineering information.
  • Establishing new client; planning and organizing sales calls and visits.

Requirements

  • Male / Female.
  • Diploma / Bachelor Degree (D3 / S1) of Electrical Engineering.

Sales Representative

Job Description

  • Pitching for new client/project opportunity.
  • Giving sales presentations.
  • Planning schedule to call or visit existing or potential sales clients, and submitting activity and results reports.
  • Resolving customer complaints.

Requirements

  • Diploma / Bachelor Degree (D3 / S1) of any fields.
